FIS-OT: Feature-Induced Optimal Transport for Unsupervised Action Segmentation

Linxiang Peng, Xinyao Qin, Jinhan Li, Di Yang, Jiangtao Wang

ICME 2026

We proposed FIS-OT, a feature-induced optimal transport framework for unsupervised action segmentation. By coupling local representation learning with global structural alignment in a cyclic optimization process, FIS-OT overcomes the rigid priors and confirmation bias present in prior OT-based methods.
